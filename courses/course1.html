Course Overview

A comprehensive beginner-level course in commercial shipping

"Introduction to Commercial Shipping" is a beginner-friendly program crafted to provide a strong foundation in the dynamic world of global maritime trade. This course offers a complete overview of how the commercial shipping industry operates, who its key players are, and why it remains central to the global economy — handling over 85% of world trade.

Learners will explore the distinctions between technical and commercial shipping, understand stakeholder roles, vessel types, and cargo classifications, and gain insights into the economic significance of the $3 trillion shipping sector. A dedicated module on the International Maritime Organization (IMO) highlights the regulatory backbone of the industry.

Additionally, the course introduces a hands-on AI-based simulator training, helping learners grasp real-world commercial scenarios in a tech-driven environment.
