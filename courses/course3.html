Course Overview

Pre-advanced training for learners ready to master real-world chartering operations and negotiations.

This course is designed for learners who already have a foundational understanding of commercial shipping and are ready to advance their skills in the dynamic world of ship brokering and chartering. Through a blend of theory, practical exercises, and AI-based simulation training, you will explore the deeper workings of the chartering market, master key contractual terms, and gain confidence in handling real-world fixture scenarios. You will learn how brokers operate in competitive global markets, how freight rates are calculated, how laytime is assessed, and how voyage estimation impacts deal-making. The course also covers common charter party disputes, resolution strategies, and the latest trends shaping the shipping industry, including the role of digitalisation and artificial intelligence. By the end of the program, you will be equipped with the practical knowledge and analytical tools to negotiate effectively, assess risks, and contribute strategically in ship brokering and chartering transactions.
