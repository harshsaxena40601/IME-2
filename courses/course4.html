Course Overview

Advanced training to refine your expertise in ship brokering and chartering with industry-specific applications.

This advanced-level program is designed for professionals and maritime enthusiasts who wish to deepen their expertise in the complex world of ship brokering and chartering. Covering in-depth concepts, industry-specific terminologies, and real-world applications, this course equips learners with the skills to navigate high-value negotiations, interpret intricate charter party agreements, and make data-driven commercial decisions. From mastering laytime calculations and voyage estimations to analyzing freight rates and market trends, participants will gain a strong command over both theoretical and practical aspects of advanced chartering. Special focus is given to dispute resolution, break-even freight analysis, and the use of AI-based simulators to replicate real-world brokering scenarios, ensuring an industry-relevant learning experience.
