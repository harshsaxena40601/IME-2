Course Overview

Foundation-level program covering essential concepts and practical skills in ship brokering and chartering.

This foundation-level program is designed to equip learners with a strong understanding of ship brokering and chartering—two essential pillars of the global maritime industry. The course covers both theoretical concepts and practical insights, from the roles and responsibilities of a ship broker to the different types of chartering agreements. Learners will gain exposure to real-world industry practices, master key abbreviations and terminologies, and explore the benefits and challenges of operating in the maritime market. The program also integrates AI-based simulator training, enabling participants to apply concepts in realistic, scenario-driven exercises for a hands-on learning experience.
