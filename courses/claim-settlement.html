Course Overview

Practical and legal aspects of maritime claim handling, supported by AI-driven simulators.

This course provides a comprehensive understanding of maritime claims and equips professionals with the practical skills to manage, assess, and settle claims efficiently. Participants will gain insights into the legal and contractual framework governing claims, including vessel arrest, jurisdictional matters, cargo damage, and preventive measures. The program emphasizes real-world application through AI-based simulators, enabling participants to practice claim calculation, documentation, and negotiation in a risk-free environment. Participants will learn to identify types and causes of damage, initiate claims effectively, and adopt proactive strategies to minimize disputes — making this course ideal for maritime professionals aiming to enhance operational efficiency and legal compliance in claim management.
